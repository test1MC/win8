EBrailler Cosmo

Current Vendor information about the EBrailler Cosmo

A brand new braille typewriter designed to work with DBT 11.2

To install the Cosmo Drivers, run d:\dbt_contents\Drivers\Cosmo\CosmoDrivers.exe from your DBT CD-ROM. These drivers are also located in a subdirectory of your DBT software: [DBT directory]\Drivers\Cosmo\CosmoDrivers.exe

Connect the USB cable between the Cosmo and your computer. When you launch DBT, DBT recognizes the Cosmo.
