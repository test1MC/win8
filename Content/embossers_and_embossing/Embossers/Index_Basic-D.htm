Index Basic-D

Current Vendor information about the Index Basic-D

The Index Basic-D is an interpoint Embosser that does graphics.

Details on interfacing this Index embosser.

Because of variations of Index firmware updates and prior line spacing settings, you may not be able to obtain as many lines per page as DBT might allow. Use care when attempting to emboss with as many lines as possible with your embosser.
