ATC Ohtsuki

The Ohtsuki embosser is a single sided embosser that does graphics. It can put inkprint and braille on the same page. The Ohtsuki requires a special type of paper that includes index holes, without which, your form feeds are not executed properly. This paper is available in the U.S. from American Thermoform. The Ohtsuki has a built-in printer for interline inkprint. The text for the inkprint is generated by a braille-to-print translator inside the hardware itself. Therefore, the accuracy of the inkprint is not attributable to this software, and the inkprint should not be used to determine the accuracy of the braille.
