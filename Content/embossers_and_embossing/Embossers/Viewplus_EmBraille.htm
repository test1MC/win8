Viewplus EmBraille

Current Vendor information about the Viewplus EmBraille

For the support of Viewplus embossers, Duxbury recommends using up-to-date drivers from the Viewplus website, and up-to-date Duxbury DBT software.

Details on interfacing this embosser.

On DBT you must set the left margin to 1 to avoid losing braille at the beginning of each line.
