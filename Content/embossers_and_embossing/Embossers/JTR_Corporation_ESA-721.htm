JTR Corporation ESA-721

Current Vendor information about the JTR Corporation ESA-721

The ESA Pro is a double sided embosser made by JTR Corporation of Japan. To do interpoint, you first print the odd pages, then rethread for the even pages. Make sure you set the switches on the machine for North American Braille.
