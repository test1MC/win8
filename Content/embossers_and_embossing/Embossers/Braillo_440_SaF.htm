Braillo 440 SWSF

Current Vendor information about the Braillo 440 S&F

The Braillo 440 SW is an interpoint embosser that is capable of printing on a double-sized (folio) page at one time. It has the equipment to stitch and fold the magazine sized braille in an automated operation.
