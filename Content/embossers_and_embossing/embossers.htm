Embossers and Embossing

Over 90 makes and models of braille embossers are supported, some dating back over 30 years, others introduced only months ago.

Before you connect your embosser to your system, we would strongly recommend doing some homework first to determine when to connect it.

If in doubt, we suggest you contact your supplier first.
