Use of the DBT Template Improvements

As you import a file, DBT asks you for a Template Name. Choose one of the DBT Templates listed to start to use one of the templates.

In most of the supplied templates we have added styles called math and TextWithinMath. For a description, see the list of mathematics improvements in What's New in 11.3?.

To see the styles in a DBT Template press F8 in the DBT Editor (this is the same as Layout: Apply Style).
