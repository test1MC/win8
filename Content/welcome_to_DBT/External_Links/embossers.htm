Use of the Braille Embosser Improvements

Most of these items require the use of the mentioned embosser.

To use Tactile View software, you must obtain the Tactile View software (like DBT, this costs money).

Many embossers from Enabling have the feature called ET Speaks. In certain situations in prior versions, ET Speaks would verbalize unwanted material. Now DBT and ET Speaks are better co-ordinated, resulting in more appropriate output.
