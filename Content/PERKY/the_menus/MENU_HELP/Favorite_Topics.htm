Help: Favorite Topics...

Keystroke: f (from menu)

You may find Topics which you wish to refer to on a regular basis. If you add Topics to your Topical Favorites in the normal way, you may use this menu shortcut to get to them quickly at a later stage.

Help: Check for Updates...

Keystroke: r (from menu)

In due course, there may be updates released for Perky Duck. Selecting this option will start your web browser, which will in turn go to our Update Web site, where it will advise you if any updates are available.
