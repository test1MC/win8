Welcome, Activated User

Your program is licensed and activated, which is the primary purpose of the Activation Wizard.

However, you can also use the wizard to collect diagnostic information, or to perform other tasks. You can do any of the following:

  • Registration -- telling us who you are.
  • De-activation -- removing the activation on one computer, so you can install on another computer.
  • Diagnosis of activation problems.
  • Connection to a shared activation computer (a.k.a, network license host) for multi-user licenses.
