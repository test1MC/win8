Registration, User Contact Information

Here you enter your contact information.

If you do not provide a First and Last Name, then you must enter an Organization Name, and vice versa.

If your country does not use postal codes, you can enter NONE.

You are not required to enter a Telephone number, but it is recommended. If you do, please follow our suggested formats:

  • 303-555-1212 (USA or Canada: just 10 digits and 2 hyphens, no spaces, no parentheses, and nothing before the area code)
  • 303-555-1212 ext. 303 (USA or Canada with an extension)
  • +44-123-456-7890 (United Kingdom: plus sign indicating international access, then country code, then space, then the national number in the format you desire)
  • +961 3-777777 (Lebanon: same idea, plus sign, then country code, then space, then the national number)
