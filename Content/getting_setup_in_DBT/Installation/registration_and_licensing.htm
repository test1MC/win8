Licensing, Registration and Activation

If you have arrived at this topic having clicked on Help: Registration, it usually means that you are not connected to the Internet. (Or you might simply have selected the Topic from the Table of Contents on the left)

For full details on the process are contained in Licensing, Registration and Activation.

We would strongly recommend you register your version of DBT at the earliest possible opportunity. Product support cannot be provided otherwise.

If you are unable to connect to the Internet, you may access a printable Registration form here by clicking on the Registration Form link. You may then complete the form on screen and print it, or alternatively print and complete it by hand.

You may then fax or mail the form to Duxbury, or your local dealer, or even telephone either, to obtain Activation details.

You may however also register on the Internet at http://www2.duxburysystems.com/registration.asp but bear in mind that you will need to know your serial number to complete the form as it is not picked up from DBT.
