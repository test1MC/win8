Will I need to upgrade my braille embosser?

Not normally unless your embosser has built-in translation tables, and certainly not if you are using DBT

Provided your embosser works with your current computer,and its operating system, you will not have to upgrade the firmware unless specifically advised by the manufacturer.

However, if you are upgrading your computer to a later operating system, do contact the embosser manufacturer to ensure that there are drivers available.
