Do I need to show bold, underline, italics, etc.?

"Yes" and "No" is the answer here.

However, in many documents features like this are often what we might call "eye candy", and simply there to make the document visually appealing.

On the other hand, it is possible that they do have a purpose or meaning. For example text might say:

"Suggest an alternative adjective for words which are underlined".

In this example, the italics are clearly decorative, but the braille reader will need to be able to identify underlined words.
