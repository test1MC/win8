Character Code Table - (U+03xx) - Greek and Coptic

Note: The following table may not display all characters in the Char column on your system. This is entirely dependent on the version of Windows you are running, and what Character Sets are installed.

Support for Greek characters is found on Windows XP and all subsequent editions of Windows.

The Greek translation tables support Greek characters. The translators that support braille math codes also support the major Greek letters.
