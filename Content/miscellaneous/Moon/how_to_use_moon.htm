How To Use DBT's Print To Moon Translation Facility.

  1. Begin a new print document, selecting either "English Moon) - basic" or "English Moon - basic no capitals" as the Template..
  2. Type text into your document as you normally would.
  3. Use the existing formatting styles and codes to format your Moon document.
  4. Use the special Moon codes to translate your document to Moon
  5. Translate to moon by selecting "File: Translate" or pressing Ctrl + t.
  6. Although you have translated to Moon at this point, you will see braille on the screen. You may have better results by selecting "View: Print Font" from the menu or pressing Ctrl+F5.
  7. You may now emboss the Moon to an Enabling Technologies embosser set to Moon Mode (or equivalent).
