View: Braille Font

Keystroke: Ctrl + F6

Keystroke: ⌘F6

By choosing the Braille Font view, your documents will appear on-screen as graphical dots. Unless you are viewing the codes, a braille document shown with Braille Font is WYSIWYGWYSIWYG. In other words, your embosser should produce exactly what you see on-screen.
