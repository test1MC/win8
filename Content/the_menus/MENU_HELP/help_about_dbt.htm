Help: About DBT

Keystroke: a from Help menu

There are two very important items on this screen which you will require if you contact Duxbury or your Duxbury dealer for any reason. The Version number and date which may follow, and the license number.

If you are using a screen reader, simply pressing the tab key will allow you to hear the Version and License Number.
