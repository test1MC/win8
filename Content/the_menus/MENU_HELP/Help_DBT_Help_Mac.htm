Help: DBT Help

Keystroke: None; select from menu.

Selecting this menu item opens DBT Help, starting at the first level of a multi-level table of contents, which allows you to navigate to your area of interest, and then "drill down" from there.

The help system is extensive, and based on years of experience with DBT on the Microsoft Windows platform. If you find a topic that still holds Windows-centric information, kindly be patient as we bring all our DBT for Mac documentation up to date.

The Help window remains in the foreground as long as it is open. To close Help, click on the standard window close button in the top left corner.
