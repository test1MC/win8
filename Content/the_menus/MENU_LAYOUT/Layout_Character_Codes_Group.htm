Layout: Character Codes: Group

Keystroke: Ctrl + b

Keystroke: ⌘B

Group is used to treat an entire highlighted blockblock as one word for flush right and other commands. Although the effect is the same, grouping is easier than placing a hard spacehard space between each word in the block.

Note: Be careful not to group too large a block; even though the text may easily fit on one print line, it may require two or more braille lines.
