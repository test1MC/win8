Unified English Braille (UEB)

The most major change to English braille has been the adoption of Unified English Braille code (UEB) by the world's major English speaking countries.

The single most beneficial aspect of UEB is a code which is the same wherever produced.

It should be stressed at the outset however, that UEB is a braille Code only. It specifically does not affect any braille layout recommended or advised by national braille authorities.
