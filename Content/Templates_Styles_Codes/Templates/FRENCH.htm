French  

Modern day French is now Unified. That is to say the Braille Code should now be the same wherever French braille is used.

Here is a list of the four DBT Templates for French:

Francais 2006 - abrege = Contracted braille.

Francais 2006 - abrege_braille_de_base = Contracted braille, without emphasis. (emphasis in print is not shown in the braille)

Francais 2006 - integral = Uncontracted braille.

Francais 2006 - integral_braille_de_base = Uncontracted braille, without emphasis. (emphasis in print is not shown in the braille)
