Centering

Code Used: [hds] and [hde]

Keystroke: Shift + F6

What does it do?

It centers text, but ensuring that at least one line of text follows. (Similar in principal to "Widows and Orphans")

Where would it be used?

To center a heading. Note that by default, at least one line must be available following the heading, otherwise the heading will appear on a new page.

Usage in DBT:

[hds]This is my heading.[hde]
It is followed by regular text.
