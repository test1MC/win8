Skip to (from page end)

Code Used: [sk-1]

Keystroke: (None at present)

See Also: Footers

What does it do?

It forces what follows to a specified number of lines from the end of the page. i.e. 1 is the last line, 2 is the second last line, etc.. This specifically excludes Footer Lines.

Usage in DBT:

This could be line 5[<]
[sk-1]
Document ends.

Produces in Braille:

(The words "Document ends." would appear on the last body text line of the page - i.e. excluding any Footer Lines.)
