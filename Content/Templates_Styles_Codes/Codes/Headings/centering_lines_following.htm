Centering + Lines Following

Also known as: Widows and Orphans.

(See also: Centering and Centering + Lines Before)

Code Used: [hdsN] + [hde]

Keystroke: (None at present)

What does it do?

It centers text horizontally on the current page, provided N + 1 lines are available on the rest of the page. It breaks to the next page and centers the text otherwise.

Where would it be used?

Where you wish control over how many lines must follow the heading before a new page.
