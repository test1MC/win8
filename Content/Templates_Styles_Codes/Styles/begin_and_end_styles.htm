Begin and End Styles.

What you need to know first: How to insert a Code or a Style.

(See also: End Style)

Codes Used: [es~E] [ee~E]where E is the Style required

Keystroke: (None at present)

What do they do?

They indicate the beginning and end of a Style.

Where would it be used?

To indicate the beginning and end of a Style

Usage in Duxbury:

[es~h1.]Chapter 1[ee~h1.]

Produces in Braille:

          Chapter #a

Let us explain!

The [es~h1.] indicates the beginning of the DBT h1. Style, which is effectively a main heading. [ee~h1.] indicates the end of the Style.
