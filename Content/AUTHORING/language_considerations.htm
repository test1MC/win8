Language Considerations

Currently a number of Duxbury products are available where the on-screen contents such as menus, prompts and dialogues are in a non-English language.

However, Help is usually in English, and users often rely on a Translation Engine to translate content into their own language.

There is however an option for Flare called "Lingo" - (http://www.madcapsoftware.com/products/lingo/). The current (July 2016) cost of this add-on is $798. This would allow interested parties to translate Help into their own language.

Should anyone be interested, please do not quote any costings until a fuller estimate of the work required has been done.

Golden Rules

  • 1) Avoid the use of apostrophes.
  • 2) Avoid the use of coloquialisms.
  • 3) If technical terms require to be used, try to explain as simply as possible.
