To Activate DBT

There are two places where you may activate your program. The first is the initial splash screen which appears when you run DBT.

The second is from the Help: About DBT... dialog. (See "Help: About DBT".)

When you click on either "Activate" button, you will be taken into the Activation Wizard.

The Activation Wizard leads you through the process of Activating and Registering DBT on your computer. It works most easily through an internet connection, but offers you other ways to activate if you do not have, or cannot use, your internet connection.
