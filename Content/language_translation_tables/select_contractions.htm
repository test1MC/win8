What, if anything, appears here will depend on the language you have chosen. Essentially this will usually be one of the English Languages.

Currently there are three Graduated tables following braille teaching courses.

Fingerprint from the Royal National Institute of the Blind, United Kingdom.

NLS - National Library Service for the Blind, United States

TSVBI - Texas School for the Blind and Visually Impaired, United States

These allow you to select a Translation Table which only contains certain contractions according to the Unit, Section or Cluster the student has reached in learning braille.
