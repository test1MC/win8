Save DBT to Word

  • You need to open an inkprint DBT file (a *.dxp file).
  • You need to have a copy of Microsoft Word (2007 or higher) installed on your computer.
  • From the DBT File Menu, select Save as...
  • From the drop-down"Save as type:" list, select Word Document (*.docx)

  • After a short delay, your Word file will have been created.

NOTE: Please be aware that you should check your Word document carefully for Styles applied and layout in general.
